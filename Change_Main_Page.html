ELEVATE WITH COMMUNITY


Purpose


Through careful observation of the data, B.L.A.C.C. can make educated decisions on what aspects may need improve in the present; and the ability to foresee damaging circumstances that can be minimized before calamity occurs. Monitoring of B.L.A.C.C. owned parcels and having data on B.L.A.C.C. acres will allow for calculative development.


There are eight databases with multiple subcategories, whiich will provide the necessary components to view important sectors that alter/influence parcels to provide a better quality of life.

About


The quality of the community that individuals decide to inhabit should have the necessary components to provide a better quality of life. The collaborative nature of the BLACC Database with BLACC Connect inspired Asclepieion Of BLACC


After intense reserch for 5 yeasr, BLACC has identified eight main categories that are necessary in order to build a well-rounded community. These eight categories are Food/Water/Clothing/Housing, Donate Outgoing, Donate Receiving, Entertainment, Monetary Gains, Resources, Advisory/Counseling and transportation.


For the security of the data that is presented within Asclepieion Of BLACC, access to the data input is monitored by BLACC Connect officers. BLACC members will be able to view the data and perform hypothetical calculations to see possible outcomes.
