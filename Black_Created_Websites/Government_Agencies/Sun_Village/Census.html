SVTC Sun Village Census




The Sun Village California Census is to be completed by every adult within the jurisdiction of Sun Village, California.








Article 3 - Jurisdiction




Section 1.



The Sun Village Town Council shall preside over the area so known as the Sun Village Community.



Section 2.




Town Boundary:







From the intersection of the city of Palmdale boundary with 70th Street East and Avenue P-8 to 80th Street East; South on 80th Street East to Avenue Q; East on Avenue Q to 170th Street East; South on 170th Street East to Palmdale Blvd; East on Palmdale Blvd. to 215th Street East; South on 215th Street East to Avenue T; West on Avenue T to 165th Street East; South on 165th Street East to Avenue T-4; West on Avenue T-4 to 106th Street East; North on106th Street East to Avenue T; West on Avenue T to 70th Street East; North on 70th Street East following the city of Palmdale boundary Northeasterly to the intersection of 70th Street East and Avenue P-8. ​[SVTC By-Laws]
