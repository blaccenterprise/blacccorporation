B.L.A.C.C. ACADEMY BA

Vision

B.L.A.C.C. Academy is designed to teach the youth basic knowledge within various fields that are vital for innovation and cognitive accessiblility, based on Stevie W. Carpenter teaching and life experiences. B.L.A.C.C. Academy will demostrate these methods of utilizing basic knowldge within society, which will be utilized by the the youth as building blocks for critical thinking on future endevours. B.L.A.C.C. Academy visions that the youth will be able to formulate their reality differently due to how the basic knowledge was taught following Stevie W. Carpenter Curriculum; making them better equipped to deal with vast areas of life. Core belief and faith of B.L.A.C.C. Academy remains in these five mediums: Love. Information. God. Heart. Teaching.
