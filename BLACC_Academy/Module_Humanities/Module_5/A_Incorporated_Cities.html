A. Incorporated Cities BA

Takeaway

The Importance of this module is to undertand the difference between an incorporated city and unincorporated commuunities.

Summary

An incorporated city is a region governed by a local municipal corporation. Understanding the meaning of municipal corporation is vital; municipal is an adjective which means relating to a city, town or its governing body and corporation means a group of people elected to govern a city, town, or borough. So, defining a local municipal corporation would translate to a local group of elected individuals that dictate the interactions and engagements of their respective community. A list of incorporated cities will be stationed on the left-side of the profile page. The heading of each tab will have the State, County and District, to help identify what cities are within the respective State's County's District.
